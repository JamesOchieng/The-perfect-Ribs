Grilling The perfect Ribs!

Start by finding the right meat

  • Good meat coverage.
  • Avoid meat that is close to the bone.
  • avoid frozen meat or was previously frozen
  • Avoid buying Enhanced meat

Seasoning the meat

Combine the brown sugar, chili powder, 1 tablespoon salt, 1 teaspoon black pepper, the oregano, cayenne, garlic powder and onion powder in a small bowl and rub the mixture on both sides of the ribs. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour or overnight.

Prepaering the grill and cooking the meatt

Preheat a clean grill to medium heat (about 200Â°F), then oil the grill. By the way, this is how to best clean your grill. Place the ribs right on the grill, using tongs to maneuver them into place. Grill, covered, over indirect medium heat for 30 minutes on each side.
